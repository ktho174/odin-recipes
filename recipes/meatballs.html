Greek Meatballs

Description

This Greek meatballs recipe is easy to make, perfectly moist and tender, and bursting with fresh and zesty flavors.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Panko
  • Eggs
  • Greek yogurt
  • Tahini
  • Lemon

Directions

  1. Mix thhe panko, eggs, and beef
  2. Form the meatballs
  3. Bake 15-20 minutes at 425 degrees
  4. Combine yogurt, tahini, and lemon to create yogurt sauce
  5. Serve meatballs over cous cous and drizzle sauce over top
