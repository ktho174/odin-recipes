Thai Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

This Thai-inspired chicken noodle soup recipe is easy to make with whatever protein, veggies, and noodles you happen to have on hand and always tastes so fresh and flavorful!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Carrot
  • Bell Pepper
  • Chicken stock
  • Red curry paste
  • Coconut milk
  • Noodles

Directions

  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions
  2. Simmer chicken, vegetables, curry paste in the stock
  3. Shred chicken
  4. Add remaining Ingredients
  5. Serve
