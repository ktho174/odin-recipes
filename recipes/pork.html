Ginger Lime Pork

Description

This Ginger Lime Pork with Coconut Rice recipe comes together in just 30 minutes and is bursting with sweet and savory flavors.

Ingredients

  • Ground Pork
  • Grated Ginger
  • Light Brown Sugar
  • Lime Juice
  • Sriracha
  • Scallion

Directions

  1. Saute Aromatics
  2. Brown the ground pork in the same pan
  3. Add brown sugar, lime, and sriracha
  4. Season to taste
  5. Serve over coconut rice
